Grounded fairy dust

What exactly is it?

The grounded remains of fairies who perished in the 100 year war.

Ingredients

  • Glitter
  • Rainbows
  • The souls of the damned
  • Fairy corpses

Steps

  1. Pound the corpses into a fine dust
  2. Mix in the glitter
  3. Bake at 250 degrees Celcius for 40 minutes
  4. Consume their flesh as you gaze upon the rainbow
