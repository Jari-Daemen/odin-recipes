World's best lasagna

What exactly is it?

As the child of Italian immigrants to the US, I have always been blessed to be able to eat what I have dubbed the World's best lasagna. I will share with you the ingredients and instructions necessary to make it yourself!

Ingredients

  • 5 gloves of garlic
  • 750 grams of beef
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • A pinch of love

Steps

  1. Mix the beef into a fine paste
  2. Add in the salt and garlic
  3. Bake at 250 degrees Celcius for 40 minutes
  4. Enjoy your meal!
